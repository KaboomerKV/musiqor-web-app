About

MUSIQOR

Musiqor is a space which assists in introducing endless opportunities to musicians with different experiences. With the help of Musiqor connecting with individual artists along with record labels is now undemanding. It’s user-friendly UI succeeds in the app being simple to use yet intuitive and visually pleasing.

Features of Our App

Expression & Connection

Profile tools lets you customize your bio and profile photo. Artist Pick and featured playlists lets you choose a release or playlist to feature at the top of you profile, or even add a fundraising link.

Publishing Analytics

Gives everyone including songwriters and publishers stats, including streams via cover versions and remixes.

Data & Analytics

Get in-depth with stats with all the numbers you need that detail exactly how you're performing, how you're being discovered and where you audience is. Real-time stats lets you see player by player for new releases.

Teams & collaboration

Musicians, labels, managers and more can all collaborate on Musiqor. This brings everyone together with assignable roles for each users.

SoundBetter

Easily find and hire music producers right within Musiqor to work on your next track.

Talk to Artists

Get to know about artists and how where to contact and collaborate with them.
